Privacy Policy

All-In-One Tambola & Housie  ·  Last updated: June 17, 2026
In short: All-In-One Tambola & Housie uses Firebase to let you host and join real-time games with other players. We collect a minimal anonymous identity, your chosen display name, and your in-game activity (tickets, points balance, claims) — never your real name, email, or phone number unless you choose to put that information in your display name yourself.

1. Who we are

All-In-One Tambola & Housie ("the App", "we", "us") is developed and published by an independent developer. For any questions about this policy or the App, contact us at factictionary@gmail.com.

2. Information we collect

2.1 Account identity

The App uses Firebase Anonymous Authentication. When you first open the App, a random, unique identifier is created for your device — we do not ask for your name, email address, or phone number to use the App. You choose a display name, which is visible to other players in any room you join or host.

2.2 Gameplay data

We store the following in our Firebase database (Cloud Firestore) so the game can work in real time across devices:

  • Your display name
  • Your points balance and a transaction history (ledger) of point credits, ticket purchases, and prize payouts
  • Rooms you've hosted or joined, including room codes, ticket assignments, and game results
  • Your tickets for each game, including which numbers you've marked
  • Prize claims you make during a game

2.3 Advertising data

The App displays ads via Google AdMob, which may collect a device advertising identifier and related technical data to serve and measure ads. See Section 6 for details.

2.4 What we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect your real name, email address, or phone number (unless you voluntarily include such information in your chosen display name, which we recommend against)
  • We do not access your contacts, precise location, photos, or files
  • The App's camera permission is used solely to scan a QR code to join a room — we do not store or transmit any camera images
  • We do not process real-money payments of any kind within the App

3. How we use your information

We use the information described above to:

  • Let you host and join real-time multiplayer games
  • Display your tickets, the called numbers, and prize claims to you and to other players in the same room
  • Maintain your points balance across games
  • Show advertisements to support the free version of the App
  • Diagnose and fix technical issues

4. How your information is shared

4.1 With other players

Your display name, ticket numbers, marked numbers, and prize claims are visible to other players in the same game room. This is necessary for the App's core multiplayer functionality — tambola is a shared, social game by nature.

4.2 With the host of a room

If you join a room, the host can see your display name, your ticket requests, and your points balance to the extent necessary to approve your ticket purchase. Hosts can also manually credit points to your balance after receiving payment from you outside the App (for example, in cash or via a payment app of your choosing). We do not process, store, or have visibility into any actual payment details — only the resulting point amount the host enters.

4.4 We do not sell your data

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Data retention and deletion

We retain your gameplay data for as long as your anonymous account is active, so you can keep your points balance and game history across sessions. If you uninstall the App, your local session ends, but your data may remain in our Firebase database tied to your anonymous identifier unless you request deletion.

To request deletion of your data, email us at factictionary@gmail.com with your in-app user ID (visible in the App's account/profile screen) and we will delete your associated records from our database within a reasonable time, except where we are required to retain certain records (for example, to investigate abuse or fraud, or to comply with legal obligations).

6. Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display banner and rewarded video advertisements. AdMob may collect your device's advertising identifier and other technical information to serve ads that may be personalized based on your interests, and to measure ad performance.

You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time through your device settings (on Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads, or equivalent, depending on your device and OS version). Opting out will not stop ads from showing, but ads may be less relevant to you.

7. Children's privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App's anonymous authentication and player-visible display names mean we have no reliable way to verify age; if you believe a child has provided us with information through the App, please contact us at factictionary@gmail.com and we will take appropriate action, including deleting such information.

8. Data security

All communication between the App and our Firebase backend is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS. We use Firebase Security Rules to restrict who can read or write specific pieces of game data (for example, only a room's host can approve ticket requests, and only a ticket's owner can mark numbers on it). No security measure is perfect, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

9. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (see Section 5)
  • Object to or restrict certain processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at factictionary@gmail.com.

10. International data transfers

Our backend infrastructure (Firebase) is operated by Google and may store and process data in data centers located in different countries than your own. By using the App, you understand that your information may be transferred to and processed in countries with different data protection laws than your country of residence.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by an updated "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

12. Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or the App, please contact us at factictionary@gmail.com.
